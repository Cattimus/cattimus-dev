Setting up your own email

Last updated: 7-10-2023

Have you ever worried about losing access to your email?
Have you ever felt like your digital life is at the mercy of large megacorporations that don't care?
If you answered yes to either of those, you might want to set up your own email!

This is a guide on how to go about setting up email with your own custom domain and how to set up with a paid email service. Additionally, some of the footguns you might run into are covered.

Preventing email spoofing

Last updated: 7-10-2023

This is an overview on SPF, DKIM, and DMARC. These are tools used to prevent users from receiving emails that are pretending to come from your domain. They won't prevent you from receiving spam, that is the job of you and your email provider. However, it is important to make sure other people aren't being fooled into believing you're sending emails that you're not.

You may also use the alternate approach of signing your own emails, but this is more complicated and should be used in addition to the protections provided by SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.
